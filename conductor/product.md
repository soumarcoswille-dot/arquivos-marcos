# Product Context: Semente Hub & iGreen Energy

## Semente Hub
- **Objective**: Intelligent SDR (Sales Development Representative) automation.
- **Goal**: Automate initial contact with leads from digital sources via WhatsApp using AI.
- **Key Feature**: "Origem" filter (digital = auto-reply, manual = silent).
- **Pipeline**: Novos Leads -> Em Qualificação -> Reunião -> Negociação -> Pagamento -> Licenciado.

## iGreen Energy
- **Objective**: Gamified recruitment funnel for licensees.
- **Goal**: Educate leads through a "Marketing of Diagnosis" and funnel them into the automation workflow.
- **Aesthetic**: Premium, Apple-inspired, high conversion.
